What can I do with UnderLX?

With UnderLX, you can:

  • Check the current and past service status (disturbances);
  • Find out your location inside the subway network;
  • Compute the shortest route between stations;
  • Check your trip history, as logged by this app;
  • See the network map;
  • See info about each station;
  • Read the titles of the official announcements published on the subway website;
  • Check network stats as calculated by our service.
