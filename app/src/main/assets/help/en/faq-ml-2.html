How much does a ticket to ride the Lisbon Metro cost?

Occasional titles:

Carris/Metro single ticket: 1,50€ (valid for only one trip in all the Lisbon Metro and Carris networks, during 1 hours after the initial validation)

Carris/Metro 24h ticket: 6,40€

Carris/Metro/Transtejo 24h ticket: 9,50€

Carris/Metro/CP 24h ticket: 10,55€

Zapping: 1,33€

Viva Viagem card: 0,50€

Bank card:

VIVA Go: 1,50€

Caixa VIVA: 1,33€

Monthly fares:

Navegante Municipal: 30,00€ (valid for unlimited trips on all public transport of the city of Lisbon)

Navegante Urbano: 40,00€ (valid for unlimited trips on all public transport of the Lisbon Metropolitan Area)
