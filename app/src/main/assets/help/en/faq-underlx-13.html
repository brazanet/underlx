How can I help UnderLX?

By installing the app and recording your travels you are already helping us by providing precious information to improve our service.

Since we want to maintain our independence and neutrality, and avoid the use of advertising, we depend on donations to support our project. We have been working in it in a dedicated way for over a year, in our spare time, in a total of thousands of hours of work. This project has real costs associated with, for example, Internet hosting, trips for application testing, etc.

If you want to help us and be part of this project, while receiving exclusive rewards, you can make us a small monthly contribution in our Patreon.

Alternatively, you can check here how to help us.

Your contribution will motivate us and enable us to continue to develop the entire UnderLX, ensuring the continuity and independence of the project.
